About Us

History

In 1986, Reverend Pastor Mary and Reverend Pastor Joseph Henry left their hometown, Rajahmundry, answering God’s call to spread His word. With unwavering faith, they moved to Hyderabad with their three young children, trusting in His greater purpose. Despite early challenges, their dedication never wavered. Small gatherings at home soon grew into a thriving church, built on a foundation of prayer, compassion, and service. Today, their legacy lives on through Rev. Pastor Samuel, who continues to preach the gospel, lead the congregation, and share God’s love with communities far and wide.

Our Mission

To bring hope, healing, and new beginnings to the unreached around the world.
We are committed to sharing God’s love through prayer, support, and encouragement for those who have yet to hear His message.
Our goal is to build a global community of faith, restore dignity, and inspire brighter futures through the power of prayer and connection.

Our Vision

At Immanuel Ministries, we are dedicated to reaching the lost and helping those in need through the power of God's love. Our vision is to:

  • Bring hope and healing to the sick and dying through prayer and the Word of God.
  • Serve the poor and marginalized, providing not only for their physical needs but also guiding them toward spiritual transformation.
  • Share the love of Christ with every person, offering salvation and restoration through the Gospel.
  • Empower individuals and families to experience the fullness of life that comes through faith in Christ.

We believe that through prayer, compassion, and outreach, God will transform lives and raise up a community of believers who live in His purpose and power.
